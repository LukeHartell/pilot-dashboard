Request All Your Data

You can request a copy of all personal information stored in Pilot Dashboard. This includes your account details, any planes you've added, your flights, and all logbook entries. Once you request your data, a report will be generated and sent to your registered email address. The process may take a few minutes depending on how much data you have.

Passwords are not included in the export. They are stored as salted SHA-256 hashes, and even these hashes are left out for security reasons. This helps keep your account safe and prevents exposure of details about how passwords are protected.
